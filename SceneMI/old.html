Robustness on (Noisy) Keyframes

Our work demonstrates robustness to keyframe quality by effectively handling imperfections and noise. We provide video comparisons with baselines for scene-aware motion in-betweening under noisy keyframe conditions, using a synthetic test set created by adding Gaussian noise to the TRUMANS dataset. Additionally, we visualize performance in dense noisy keyframe settings (interval of 3), highlighting robustness in extreme scenarios. The videos are switchable, allowing viewers to observe how noisy keyframes are given as input.
