3. Comparisons to Previous Works

We provide video comparisons with state-of-the-art online (RPM) and offline (UniEgoMotion) baselines for egocentric motion reconstruction.

- Online methods (RPM) struggle to handle abrupt changes in egocentric signals and typically have limited model capacity, resulting in lower motion quality.

- Offline methods (UniEgoMotion) achieve higher motion fidelity but violate causal constraints and rely on window-based generation and stitching for long-term motion reconstruction, which introduce temporal discontinuities.

In contrast, EgoForce generates globally coherent, smooth, and long-term stable motion while strictly satisfying causal constraints, achieving state-of-the-art reconstruction performance.
