Inwoo Hwang

I'm currently a 3rd year PhD student in 3D Vision lab at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Young Min Kim. My research is focused on building practical applications for 3D data, spanning the fields of computer vision and character animation. I was a research intern in the Computational Imaging team at Snap Research.
